Tom Yum Noodles

A nostalgic comfort food I used to eat frequently as a student.

Ingredients

  • 150ml of Chicken Broth
  • 1 tsp of Tom Yum Paste
  • 60g of dried Ee Mian
  • A handful of Bok Choy
  • 30g of marinated Minced Pork
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp of chopped Green Onion

Steps

  1. To a saucepan, add chicken broth and Tom Yum Paste.
  2. Using a pair of chopsticks, stir the contents of the saucepan until the Tom Yum Paste is well mixed into the broth
  3. When the soup begins to boil, lower the heat.
  4. Add in Ee Mian, Bok Choy, and marinated minced pork.
  5. Allow the contents of the saucepan to boil for 3 minutes.
  6. After 3 minutes, crack an egg into the saucepan and allow it boil for another minute.
  7. When that's done, transfer the Tom Yum Ee Mian into a bowl (be careful with the egg!)
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions.
